# Product Glossary (Phase 1)

- **Branch**: Physical store location (MGA, MSY, RIV).
- **Physical Cash Box**: Unique branch cash register entity.
- **Cash Session**: Operational cashier shift over a cash box.
- **Sale Order**: Commercial order from draft to dispatch lifecycle.
- **Dispatch Ticket**: Warehouse fulfillment record for paid orders.
- **Approval Request**: Controlled decision workflow for exceptions (returns, etc.).
- **Transfer**: Inter-branch inventory movement request and execution.
- **WAC**: Weighted Average Cost per branch-product.
